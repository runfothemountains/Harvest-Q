📜 Farming Laws

USA: Roadside stands allowed in most states if produce is grown by seller. Cottage food laws vary.
India: Farmers’ Produce Trade and Commerce Act allows inter-state trade without middlemen.
Nigeria: Roadside vending common, but some states require local council permits.

⚕️ Farmer Medical Advice

USA: Reminder to wear gloves during pesticide application. Free checkups often available at local extension services.
India: Common issues: heat stroke & dehydration. Advice: always carry water, wear cotton clothes in fields.
Nigeria: Snake bites and malaria common. Farmers advised to keep anti-venom nearby and use mosquito nets.
